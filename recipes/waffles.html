Waffles

LEARN HOW TO MAKE A DELICIOUS PLATE OF WAFFLES

Ingredients

  • Two whole eggs - whisked until light and fluffy
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Vegetable oil
  • Tablespoon of white sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Vanillla
  • Cooking spray

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs, then add the flour, milk, and oil. Whisk in the sugar. Stir in the remaining ingredients.
  2. Pour the batter onto the hot, prepared waffle iron. Cook until golden brown and repeat with the remaining batter.
